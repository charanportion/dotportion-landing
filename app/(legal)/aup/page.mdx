# Acceptable Use Policy

lastUpdated: "09 December 2025"

To maintain a safe and reliable platform, all users must follow this policy.

## Prohibited Activities

You may NOT use DotPortion to:

- Engage in fraud, phishing, impersonation, or illegal acts
- Distribute malware, exploits, or malicious payloads
- Harass, threaten, or harm others
- Send spam or unsolicited bulk requests
- Overload or stress-test the system without permission
- Violate privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

---

## Resource Usage

You must not:

- Intentionally degrade platform performance
- Attempt to access other users' resources
- Bypass rate limits or security controls

---

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:

- Temporary suspension
- Permanent account termination
- Reporting to authorities (in severe cases)
