# Cookie Policy

lastUpdated: "09 December 2025"

This Cookie Policy explains how DotPortion uses cookies and similar technologies.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to enhance functionality, remember preferences, and analyze usage.

We may also use:

- LocalStorage
- SessionStorage
- Web beacons and pixels

---

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

Required for:

- Login sessions
- Security validation
- Core platform functionality  
  Cannot be disabled.

### Performance & Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how users interact with DotPortion.

### Preference Cookies

Store UI preferences such as theme, layout, and workflow state.

### Marketing Cookies

Used only with consent to track campaign performance and user engagement.

---

## How to Manage Cookies

You can disable cookies in browser settings.  
Disabling essential cookies may break parts of the platform.

---

## Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party tools (analytics, hosting) may set their own cookies.  
We ensure they meet privacy and security standards.
