# Data Processing Agreement

lastUpdated: "09 December 2025"

This DPA forms part of the Terms of Service between the **User (Controller)** and **DotPortion (Processor)**.

## Purpose

DotPortion processes data only to:

- Provide backend and workflow services
- Improve system reliability and performance
- Execute logic requested by the user

---

## Responsibilities

### Controller Responsibilities

- Determine the nature and purpose of processing
- Ensure data accuracy and legality

### Processor Responsibilities (DotPortion)

We will:

- Only process data under user instruction
- Maintain strong security measures
- Notify of breaches without undue delay
- Support audits and compliance needs

---

## Subprocessors

We may use approved subprocessors (cloud providers, analytics tools).  
A current list will be maintained.

---

## Data Rights

Users may request:

- Access or export of data
- Deletion of data
- Correction of inaccurate information

---

## Data Transfers

If transferring data outside the EU:

- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
- Equivalent legal safeguards are applied

---

## End of Agreement

Upon service termination, DotPortion will delete or return all user data upon request.
