# Privacy Policy

lastUpdated: "09 December 2025"

DotPortion (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides a visual backend development platform that helps users build APIs, workflows, schemas, and backend services.  
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- Name, email address, and account data
- Workspace or organization details
- Feedback, support interactions, and submitted forms

### Automatically Collected Data

- IP address, browser type, OS information
- Usage logs (workflow runs, API calls, system telemetry)
- Cookies and analytics data

### Sensitive Information

DotPortion does **not** store your application data unless explicitly configured.  
All secrets are encrypted and isolated per workspace.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We process your data to:

- Operate and maintain the DotPortion platform
- Improve workflows, reliability, and performance
- Provide onboarding, support, and product updates
- Secure the platform and enforce policies

We **never** sell user data.

---

## Data Protection

We implement:

- Encrypted secrets storage
- HTTPS everywhere
- Role-based access control
- Secure environment isolation
- Regular internal security reviews

---

## Third-Party Providers

We may use:

- Analytics providers
- Authentication services
- Cloud hosting (compute, database, storage)

They only receive data necessary to perform their services.

---

## Your Rights

You may request:

- Access to stored data
- Data deletion
- Correction or export
- Removal from communications

Contact: **support@dotportion.com**

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Significant changes will be communicated.
